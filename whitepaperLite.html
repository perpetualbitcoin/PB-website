1. Abstract

Perpetual Bitcoin (PB) is a fully immutable, trust‑less protocol designed to remedy the systemic flaws of modern crypto launches.

PB removes most Selling pressure from VP's, insider manipulation, time‑based vesting cliffs, liquidity rugs, governance capture, and sudden sell‑pressure shocks.
All tokens are minted at genesis and held in a single global Vault. A modest, predefined fraction becomes liquid at launch, and the remainder unlocks only when market conditions justify it -> never by time, and never by human discretion.

Every purchase mints a non‑transferable receipt‑tracker that records the buyer’s position and next unlock trigger.
PB is a mathematical constitution: one Vault, one supply, one set of rules, zero exceptions.

2. The Problem with Crypto

The original promise of crypto has been compromised by:

  • Admin Control > keys, multisigs, governance votes, upgrade proxies
  • Upgradable Contracts > rules can be replaced or rewritten
  • Pausable Systems > emergency stops and circuit breakers
  • Manipulable Liquidity > pools can be drained or influenced
  • Vesting Cliffs > predictable dump windows
  • Transferable Locked Positions > OTC disposal of obligations

ALL THIS IS SELLING PRESSURE! Perpetual Bitcoin is a protocol that addresses all of these issues. PB eliminates these failure modes by design.

3. PB Philosophy

PB is built on four non‑negotiable principles:

  1. Immutability > no admin, no owner, no upgrades
  2. Fairness > no discretionary rule changes, no backdoors
  3. Price‑Based Unlocks > unlocks are earned, not scheduled
  4. Non‑Transferability > locked claims and receipts are wallet‑bound

By adhering to these principles, PB preserves the original spirit of decentralization.

4. System Overview

PB’s architecture is simple and immutable:

  • PB Token > liquid ERC‑20 representation
  • PBc Claim Token > locked representation, non‑transferable (1-1 Backing PB)
  • Vault > single constitutional contract enforcing all rules
  • PBt Tracker NFT > non‑transferable receipt that records each purchase

Supply: The full supply is minted at genesis and held by the Vault. No further minting is possible.

5. Distribution & Liquidity (High‑Level)

Every purchase follows an immutable process:

  • A portion of each purchase becomes liquid immediately PB
  • The remainder is locked under protocol rules PBc
  • A tracker NFT is minted to record the position

While the Vault holds a surplus of tokens, a predefined portion of purchases can be matched by the vault and used to deepen liquidity on every purchase. Once the surplus is gone (ALL 21M in circullation), liquidity contribution stops and remaining tokens are reserved for future unlocks or redemptions.

6. Presale Architecture (Summary)

  • Limited number of presale blocks
  • Founder/Ops/Advertising/Dev allocation reserved
  • Remaining blocks open to the public, capped per address
  • Presale positions convert into standard allocations at launch

Full parameters and mechanics are published at launch.

7. Security Model

PB is designed to be uncheatable:

  • No admin keys
  • No upgradeability
  • No governance capture
  • Non‑transferable locked positions
  • Immutable recovery/inheritance rules

8. Philosophy & Legacy

PB is a correction, not just another token. It embodies sovereignty, immutability, fairness, transparency, long‑term alignment, and mathematical truth. Designed to outlive its creators, PB includes recovery and inheritance mechanisms so positions survive hacks, lost keys, and generational succession.

9. Conclusion

Perpetual Bitcoin is a new class of protocol: price‑based, immutable, uncheatable, and fair. It removes the manipulation vectors that have plagued crypto and replaces them with a single, transparent system that cannot be altered.

Full technical documentation is published at launch.
